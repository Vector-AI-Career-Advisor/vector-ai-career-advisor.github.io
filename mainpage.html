Career Growth Advisor

An AI-powered platform for data-driven career planning

The Problem: Tracking the Evolving High-Tech Jobs Market

Artificial intelligence systems, particularly large language models (LLMs), struggle to remain accurate in fast-changing domains. Since they are trained on static datasets, their knowledge quickly becomes outdated after deployment.

This challenge is especially severe in the high-tech job market, where frameworks, tools, and methodologies evolve rapidly. As a result, career advice generated by LLMs may be inaccurate or misleading.

Students and career-switchers are particularly vulnerable, as they often rely on AI tools due to limited access to expert human guidance.

Solution: AI Career Growth Advisor

The proposed system is an AI-powered career advisory platform tailored for the high-tech sector. It provides personalized career guidance based on a user's CV and professional goals, grounded in real-time job market data.

By continuously processing current job postings and integrating them into the advisory process, the platform ensures that recommendations remain relevant and aligned with current industry demand.

Main System Components

Data Gathering

Collects up-to-date job postings from public APIs to ensure continuous exposure to current market demand and hiring trends.

Data Processing & Analysis

Transforms raw job data into structured formats, extracting required skills, technologies, job titles, and seniority levels while analyzing demand trends.

Knowledge Representation (RAG)

Converts analyzed insights into textual representations and embeds them into a vector database to enable retrieval-augmented generation.

User-Facing LLM

Uses retrieved market insights to generate personalized career advice, CV improvement suggestions, and customized skill acquisition roadmaps.

Learning Resources Integration

Connects recommended skills to relevant courses from platforms such as Coursera and Udemy, enabling actionable learning paths.

Front-End Interface

Provides a user-friendly interface for CV upload, AI interaction, data visualizations, and presentation of recommendations.

Existing Solutions

LinkedIn Premium

Offers limited analytics and AI profile tools but is costly and restricted to the LinkedIn platform.

Kickresume

Focuses on resume tailoring and interview preparation but lacks long-term career growth guidance.

CareerFlow AI

Helps manage job applications and profiles but does not provide data-driven skill development recommendations.

Career Dreamer

Assists with career exploration but lacks grounding in real-time job market trends and CV-based personalization.

Expected Users

The primary users are students and individuals seeking career transitions. These users often lack reliable, insider knowledge about rapidly evolving technology fields and would benefit from structured, market-driven guidance.

External Dependencies

LLM (Llama)

Serves as the core NLP engine for text analysis, summarization, CV refinement, and career guidance generation.

Job Postings API

Supplies real-time labor market data, enabling accurate trend analysis and personalized job recommendations.

Learning Platforms APIs

Provide access to relevant courses and educational materials that support the recommended skill acquisition paths.
